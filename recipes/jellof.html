jellof recipe

Description

This is one of my favorite dish.

It is called jollof rice.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Rice
  • Carrot
  • Fish
  • spices

Steps

  1. First you boil and rinse the rice
  2. Grind your spices and Tomatoes together
  3. Add your fish and meat and add plenty of water
  4. Next, you let it dry and be careful for it to not burn
