Salad Recipe

Description

This is an image of my third favorite dish

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • eggs
  • cabbage
  • vinegar
  • milk and cream

Steps

  1. Slice and wash your Tomatoes and cabbages clean
  2. wash your cabbage with vinegar
  3. Slice and keep your Cucumber and add some carrots if you like
  4. Mix all together and add your eggs on top
  5. Voila!, there you have a well prepared vegetable Salad.
